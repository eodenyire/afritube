# 📄 01 — BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (BRD)

Let’s build this like something you'd present to investors or a board.

---

## 📌 1. Executive Summary

**Product Name:** AfriTube
**Vision:**
To build Africa’s leading **multi-format digital content ecosystem** that enables creators to **publish, grow, and monetize content** across video, audio, and written media.

**Mission:**
Empower African creators with tools, visibility, and monetization opportunities tailored to African realities (low bandwidth, mobile-first users, fragmented payment systems).

---

## 🌍 2. Problem Statement

African creators face structural barriers:

### Key Problems:

* Limited monetization access compared to platforms like YouTube
* Poor revenue share and delayed payouts
* High data costs limiting content consumption
* Lack of localized discovery for African content
* Fragmented platforms (video, music, blogging separated)

---

## 💡 3. Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives:

* Build a unified platform for:

  * Video
  * Audio/music
  * Blogs

* Enable creator monetization via:

  * Ad revenue sharing
  * Subscription models (future)
  * Brand partnerships (future)

### Secondary Objectives:

* Promote African content globally
* Reduce reliance on foreign platforms
* Build Africa’s digital content economy

* Premium subscriptions

---

## 📊 6. Monetization Policy

Creators qualify when:

* ≥ 100 Subscribers
* ≥ 1,000 Watch Hours

**Logic:**

```
Eligible = (Subscribers ≥ 100) AND (WatchHours ≥ 1000)
```

### After Eligibility:

* Ads enabled on videos
* Revenue split (e.g., 70% creator / 30% platform — adjustable)
